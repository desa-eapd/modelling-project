**When:** 2008–2011 & 2015–2017

**Purpose:** Economy-wide assessment of development strategies to achieve the MDGs first, and then to back the preparation of the *2030 Strategy*, a long-term **National Development Plan**. Scenarios focused on the potential contributions of different **drivers of economic growth**, including investment in public infrastructure, productivity growth across sectors, foreign direct investment, and remittances. The analysis demonstrated how potential vulnerabilities from world economic shocks may affect growth paths (via decreased export prices and increased import prices of key commodities).

**Models:** MAMS

**Who’s involved:** Government of the Kyrgyz Republic (Office of the President, Ministry of Economy) supported by UN DESA, UNDP.
